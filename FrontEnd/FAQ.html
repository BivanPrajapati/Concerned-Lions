Frequently Asked Questions and Resources


What is BU Course Helper?

The BU Course Helper is a tool designed to help Boston University students find the next courses they can take based on the classes they’ve already completed or intro classes they can take based on their interests.



How does the website know which classes I’ve already taken?

You can click the courses you’ve completed, and the graphic will use that information and show you the next classes you’re eligible for.



Where can I find classes that match my personal interests?

You can explore the Interest Specific Search page, click on the topic tags that you're interested in, and press go to find all the intro classes you can take.



Does BU Course Helper register me for classes automatically?

No. The website is a guide to help you choose your next courses. You will still need to officially register through the BU Student registration portal here



Can I see which classes fulfill certain requirements and other specifics(e.g., Hub units)?

Yes, each class name will be linked to the BU course catalog for the information you need.



Can I save my course plan for later?

No, that will have to be done in the Schedule Builder in the BU Student Portal, but you are free to take screenshots.



What should I do if a class I want is full?

If a class is full, you can join the waitlist through BU’s registration system or use BU Course Helper to find similar alternatives..



Who can I contact for technical issues with the website?

If you encounter any problems, visit our About Us page for contact information to report an issue.
